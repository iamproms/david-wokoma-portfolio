    STEM Festival 2024

    First Place Elementary ‚Ä¢ Safe Cities

    Won First Place Elementary team in the STEM Festival 2024 with my project "Solar-Powered Water Purification System". The project focused on creating an affordable and sustainable solution for clean water access in remote areas.

    The system used solar panels to power a water pump and UV purification module, capable of purifying up to 10 liters of water per day. I designed the circuit layout and built the prototype using Arduino for automated operation.

    This project taught me about renewable energy systems, circuit design, and the importance of creating practical solutions for real-world problems. The judges particularly appreciated the focus on sustainability and affordability.
